## Demo 2 — Client Insights from Email + HubSpot + Fathom

Duration: ~6 min. Format: Pre-recorded video (sanitized real data).

**Setup (30 sec):**
"This is something I actually do. I run an agency — I have clients, calls, emails, CRM notes. The problem: the full picture of any client is scattered across three tools. Email, HubSpot, Fathom call recordings. Nobody has time to cross-reference all of it."

**The prompt (15 sec):**
"I point Claude Code at my synced data — emails, HubSpot deal notes, and Fathom transcripts for a client — and ask: give me a full picture. What are they happy about, what are they worried about, what did they ask for that we haven't delivered?"

**The payoff (3 min):**
- Client satisfaction signals vs. warning signs across touchpoints
- Promises made in calls that aren't tracked in HubSpot
- Questions asked in emails that never got answered
- Pattern: "They mentioned budget concerns in 3 separate calls but never in email — they're worried but not putting it in writing"

**The land (30 sec):**
"This is my real workflow. I run this before every client check-in. It takes 2 minutes and I walk into the meeting knowing things I would have missed. You could do the same with your clients, your investors, your partners — anyone where the context is split across tools."

- This is the most authentic demo — YOUR actual workflow
- Sanitize all client data before recording
- Hits #1 survey pain (customer communication) directly
- The "promises made in calls not tracked in CRM" finding is universally relatable
